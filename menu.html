Our Menu

Mohinga

Mohinga is considered the national dish of Myanmar. It is a flavorful fish soup served with rice noodles, often enjoyed for breakfast.

Lahpet (Fermented Tea Leaf Salad)

Lahpet is a unique Burmese salad made from fermented tea leaves mixed with various ingredients like peanuts, sesame, and dried shrimp.

Shan Noodles

Shan noodles are a popular dish from the Shan state. The dish features rice noodles served with chicken or pork, vegetables, and a spicy sauce.

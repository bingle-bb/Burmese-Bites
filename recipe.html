How to Make Mohinga

Mohinga is a delicious fish soup and is the national dish of Myanmar. Here is how you can make it at home:

Ingredients:

  • Fish fillets (catfish is common)
  • Rice noodles
  • Ginger, garlic, onions, lemongrass
  • Chili powder, turmeric, and other spices
  • Herbs (coriander, cilantro)
  • Lime wedges, hard-boiled eggs

Instructions:

1. Cook the fish fillets and prepare the broth.

2. Add the spices and herbs to the broth.

3. Cook the rice noodles separately and add them to the soup.

4. Serve with garnishes such as lime, eggs, and herbs.
